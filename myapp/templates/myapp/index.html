About Our Counting System

Our advanced AI-powered system revolutionizes palm oil production monitoring by providing real-time counting and tracking of Fresh Fruit Bunches (FFB). Using cutting-edge YOLOv8 technology running on Raspberry Pi, we deliver accurate counting with live display integration to optimize production monitoring and inventory management.
